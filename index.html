Growlith – urban balcony & terrace gardening

Welcome to Growlith, where concrete meets chlorophyll. We believe that every balcony, terrace, or rooftop can become a thriving green sanctuary — no matter how small. Our service is crafted for city dwellers who dream of fresh herbs, vibrant flowers, and a personal oasis above the streets. From design to installation and year-round care, we handle everything with a focus on sustainability, beauty, and practicality.

With over a decade of experience in urban horticulture, Growlith has transformed more than 1,200 balconies and terraces across the metropolitan area. Our team of certified gardeners and landscape architects work closely with you to create a living space that reflects your personality while improving air quality, reducing stress, and even providing organic produce. We combine modern modular systems with timeless botanical wisdom.

Our core values: ecological responsibility, creative design, and long-term partnership. Every project starts with a thorough consultation, followed by a 3D mood board, plant selection, installation, and a tailored maintenance schedule. We use only peat-free soils, recycled containers, and organic fertilizers.

Bespoke design · from sketch to bloom

Every urban garden tells a story. At Growlith, we listen to your needs: do you dream of a Mediterranean herb garden, a jungle-like retreat, or a minimalist succulent display? Our designers draft a custom layout that optimizes light, wind exposure, and your personal style. We integrate vertical green walls, railing planters, hanging baskets, and modular decking to make the most of your square footage.

  • Vertical gardens – living walls with integrated irrigation, ideal for narrow balconies.
  • Edible terraces – tomatoes, chili, strawberries, and aromatic herbs in self-watering planters.
  • Pollinator havens – lavender, echinacea, and wildflowers to attract bees and butterflies.
  • Shade & privacy screens – climbing plants like jasmine or ivy on trellises.

We use smart sensors and drip irrigation to ensure your plants thrive even when you're away. And our seasonal refresh service keeps your garden exciting all year round — think autumn cyclamen, winter heather, spring tulips, and summer geraniums. Growlith is not just a service; it's a lifestyle upgrade.

Maintenance & care – your garden, our passion

Our relationship doesn't end with installation. Growlith offers flexible maintenance packages: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits, depending on your garden's needs. Our gardeners prune, fertilize, check for pests, and adjust irrigation systems to ensure optimal health. We also provide a digital garden log with photos and care tips, so you're always in the loop.

We understand that urban gardening comes with challenges: limited sunlight, strong winds, and space constraints. That's why we select robust, adaptive plant species and use lightweight, wind-resistant containers. Our plant health guarantee means that if a plant doesn't thrive, we replace it at no extra cost within the first year.

Seasonal care calendar: spring refresh with compost and new annuals; summer irrigation checks and shading; autumn cleanup and winter protection. We also offer one-off workshops for clients who want to learn the basics of balcony gardening — from sowing seeds to harvesting.

Why Growlith? Sustainability meets style

We are proud to be a carbon-neutral operation. Every plant we use is sourced from local, peat-free nurseries, and we recycle 95% of our waste (pots, soil, and green waste).
